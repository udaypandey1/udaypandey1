Swimming Day

April 8, 2023 One-minute read
fun

I went swimming with the boys. Link to heading

And it was fun. I am quite new to swimming. I learned the basics four years ago with Dad. I was really happy to find out that I hadn’t forgotten much.

I had my meal at home before 9 am and went to hostel. There I met Sachet and Amrit. Then we walked about 30 minutes to reach Marigold Party Palace, where Samir was already waiting for us.

We paid Rs. 350 each, and then swam from 10 am to almost 1 pm. It was really fun.

After that we went to Samir’s house, which was 15 minutes away. Then we stayed there for a while. We ate delicious food that Samir’s mom cooked for us. We left at around 5 pm, and I reached home at 6 pm.

It was a day well spent.
